Crime Rates in Malaysia


An Analysis of Crime Trends and Patterns Across Various Regions in Malaysia.


Safety comparison in popular Malaysian cities.

The scatter plot highlights the safety levels in popular Malaysian cities, with Tawau showing the lowest safety rates during both day and night.

In contrast, Kangar has the highest safety level at night, while Labuan records the highest safety level during the day.

Vehicle Theft Patterns in Malaysia

Lorries represent the least number of thefts, followed by cars, while motorcycles have the highest incidence of theft.

This pattern indicates a need for targeted security measures, particularly for motorcycles, which are disproportionately affected.
