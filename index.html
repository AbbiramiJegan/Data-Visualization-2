    • Trends in Vehicle Thefts by Type Over the Years

    From 2016 to 2022, vehicle thefts in Malaysia showed a consistent decline across all types.

    Motorcycle thefts saw the steepest drop, from 34,754 cases in 2016 to 10,457 in 2022. Motorcar thefts also significantly decreased, from 10,607 to 3,271 over the same period. Lorry thefts followed a similar trend, dropping from 3,050 to 718.

    This suggests improved vehicle security or law enforcement over time.
